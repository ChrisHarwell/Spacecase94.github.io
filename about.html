About Me

I am a artist first and foremost, I love to draw, paint, sketch, color, pretty much anything that allows me to get some creative work onto paper or canvas. That being said, my canvas most recently has become the web. Being an artist, but also a very strategic and analytical thinker, I realized I needed a way to express myself in a way that I could not only display my art for the world to see but also to help others, and make a decent living. Once I discovered web development I instantly fell in love, it was a way for me to show my creativity in a modern 21st century way.
